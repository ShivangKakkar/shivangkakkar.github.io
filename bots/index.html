About

Know more about our Journey!

  • 2 August, 2021

    Inauguration

    On 2 August 2021, we created our Telegram channel with the intention of providing telegram users with helpful bots to make their work more efficient, faster, free and a lot easier. Many users liked our concept.

  • 5 August, 2021

    Our First Launch

    In August 2021, we launched our first bot. In a short period of time, many users liked it and inspired us to make more bots like that.

  • December, 2021

    1000-user mark

    Our user count exceeded 1000 in December of 2021, which made us eager to build a bot library and more such bots.

  • Today

    Still Creating Things

    Since our inauguration, we have continued to create helpful bots and many other items, and each day more and more users are joining us.
